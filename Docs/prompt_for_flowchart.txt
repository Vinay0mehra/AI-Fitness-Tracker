Here is the context and the core flows of the application:

1. User Authentication: 
- Users can sign up or log in. 
- The system uses Supabase Auth. Upon sign-up, a user record is created and a database trigger initializes their profile.

2. User Onboarding: 
- After the first login, new users must provide their details (age, weight, height, gender, and fitness goals like weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain). 
- The system calculates their Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) using the Harris-Benedict equation and sets their daily calorie targets.

3. Main Dashboard: 
- A central hub showing the user's daily progress. 
- It displays daily calorie targets versus actual calories consumed and calories burned.
- Includes data visualization charts to track progress over time.

4. AI Food Logging: 
- Users can upload a photo of their meal. 
- The application sends the image to the Google Gemini AI API, which analyzes the food item and estimates the calorie intake. 
- The estimated calories are then saved to the user's daily consumption log.

5. Manual Activity Logging: 
- Users can manually log their physical activities and workouts by entering the exercise type, duration, and calories burned.
